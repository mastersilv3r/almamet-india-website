Akshar Ghai MANAGING DIRECTOR

Akshar Ghai, born 1988 has been Managing Director of ALMAMET India since April 2013. He is taking care of the complete operations of ALMAMET India Private Limited since incorporation of the company. Mr. Ghai graduated with a degree of L.L.B from University of Pune in 2011 and thereafter worked with a law firm known as International Law Group in New Delhi till March 2013.

Shpresim Jahoja DIRECTOR

Shpresim Jahoja, born 1981 has been Director of ALMAMET India since the foundation of the company. He is guarding all operations and is mainly responsible for the financial as well as the organizational structure of the company. Mr. Jahoja graduated with a degree of LFU from University of Innsbruck in 2006 and thereafter worked for the General Consulate of Austria in Munich and later as a Senior for an auditing and M&A company in Vienna before entering the ALMAMET group. He further is in charge of the Controlling for the ALMAMET group and also a Board Member of the biggest Russian casting company of non-ferrous materials.

Bernhard Rauch DIRECTOR

Bernhard Rauch, born 1955 has been Director of ALMAMET India since the foundation of the company. He is mainly responsible for the legal as well as the financial structure of the company. Mr. Rauch graduated with a degree of LFU from University of Innsbruck in 1981. He holds various Management-Positions including CFO at ALMAMET GmbH. He is part of the ALMAMET group since the beginning.
