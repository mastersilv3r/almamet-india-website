SAFETY

ALMAMET ensures a healthy, safe working condition through explicit instructions and logical processes. Twice a year ALMAMET is the organizer of a large safety seminar where customers, suppliers, employees and other concerned person are invited. In March 2018 Almamet’s Safety Seminar was held in Romania & the upcoming safety seminar will be held in Sweden in September 2018. ALMAMET has committed itself to maintain and further develop current safety standards.
