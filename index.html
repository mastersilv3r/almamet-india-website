Desulphurization a new approach in steel Industry

Founded in 1983 , ALMAMET is a leading German Multi National company when it comes to Hot Metal and Desuplhurization in Iron and Steel and is well known for its achivemnet in the field of Metallurgy. ALMAMET INDIA is a powerful and growing company in Almamet Group. Almamet became the first and foremost organization to provide complete array of solutions for Hot Metal Desulphurization under one umbrella after taking over the ThyssenKrupp HMDS technology which has commissioned 150 desulphurization stations for torpedo and open ladles worldwide in last 50 years. The services we offer encompass Supply of Reagents, Technology & Equipments, Optimization, Revamping, Design & Engineering, Consultation and total operation & maintenance of HMDS plant. Apart from Desulphurization products, ALMAMET also supplies Magnesium Rasping, Powders & Chips to the chemical and pyrotechnical industry. We look forward to provide high end solution and hope to exceed your expectations in terms of product and service
