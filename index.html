Almamet India

Welcome to the metallurgical world of ALMAMET India Private Limited. Being part of the ALMAMET group with its head office in Germany, we look back on a history of over 30 years of engineering expertise and excellence. ALMAMET is now one of the world’s largest suppliers of reagents for Desulphurization and is well known for its achievements in the field of metallurgy. ALMAMET principal business is to consult steel plants on cost saving and also supply high quality reagents for hot metal Desulphurization based on Magnesium, Calcium Carbide and Lime to steel plants. Apart from Desulphurization products, ALMAMET also supplies Magnesium Raspings, Powders & Chips to the chemical and pyrotechnical industry. We look forward to providing you a high end solution and hope to exceed your expectations in terms of product and service.
